Skeleton Space Jumper

This is a 2D platformer in python using the Pygame engine that I made during my fall semester of 2018.

The final game consists of 3 different levels that each have their own challenge to them. The game consists of jumping from platform to platform trying to get the finish line. The first level involves standing platforms and an enemy that tries to knock you off, the second level involves horizontally moving platforms,and the third level involves vertically moving platforms.

A brief description of how to play the game: Use the arrow keys to move and jump to the next platform. Each level is finished when you get to the "Exit" sign of each level. Each level gets more difficult and tests your skills from the previous level.
